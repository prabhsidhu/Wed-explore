Event

Canada is a multicultural country so the number of events and festivals are increasing at a fast rate.The event features allow us to view all the latest event of brazilian and indian community under one roof to ease the experience for the people .

Food

A foodie is a person who has an ardent or refined interest in food and who eats food not out of hunger but due to their interest or hobby.Food feature allows people to find restaurants nearby them which provide rich,healthy and nutritious food of there community which give them the experience of their native food.

Venture

Venture is a activity which is new,exciting and difficult as it involves the risk of failure the people who want to engage in adventures and enjoy adventures.venture feature allow us to view list of adventures places in british columbia and detailed description about it.

Tranquility

The aura of tranquility comes from the calm in the world, which makes you feel you are without a care in the world.Nowadays everybody has stress in there life to avoid such situations people like to go to relaxation places.This feature which would enable user to plan various activities to set him free from stressful and busy life.
